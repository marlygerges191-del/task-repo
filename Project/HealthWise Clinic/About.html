About Our Clinic

Dedicated to your health and well-being since 2010

Who We Are

At HealthWise Clinic, we believe that healthcare is more than just treating symptoms—it is about building lasting relationships.

Our clinic was founded with the vision of blending modern medical technology with compassionate, human-centered care. We provide a safe environment where patients are heard, understood, and treated with respect.

Whether you are visiting for a routine check-up or a complex procedure, our mission is to help you lead a healthier, happier life.

Our Core Values

  • Patient First – Your health is our top priority.
  • Integrity – Honest, transparent medical advice.
  • Excellence – Continuous learning and modern equipment.
  • Compassion – Treating every patient like family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?
While we accept walk-ins for emergencies, we highly recommend booking an appointment to minimize waiting time.
What insurance plans do you accept?
We accept most major insurance providers including Allianz, AXA, and MetLife. Please contact us to verify your specific plan.
Do you treat children?
Yes! We have a dedicated Pediatrics department with specialists trained to care for infants, children, and adolescents.
What are your working hours?
We are open Saturday to Thursday from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7.
Where is the clinic located?
We have branches in Nasr City, Dokki, and Smouha. You can find the exact locations on our Contact page.
How can I get my test results?
Results can be picked up in person or sent to you via email/WhatsApp within 24-48 hours depending on the test.
