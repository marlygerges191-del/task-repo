Our Medical Services

Specialized care across multiple medical departments

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive care for heart and vascular conditions.

  • Non-invasive Cardiology
  • Interventional Cardiology
  • Heart Rhythm Disorders

Pediatrics

Compassionate care for infants, children, and adolescents. We focus on physical, emotional, and social health.

Neurology

Expert diagnosis and treatment for disorders of the nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord.

Orthopedics

Specialized treatment for musculoskeletal issues, sports injuries, and joint replacements.

Stomatology (Dental)

Stomatology plays a crucial role in diagnosing and treating conditions that go beyond standard dental issues:

  • Oral Pathology: Studying diseases such as oral cancers and cysts.
  • Systemic Connections: Detecting early signs of diabetes or autoimmune disorders.
  • Jaw Disorders: Treating TMJ and facial trauma.

Ophthalmology

Comprehensive eye care services ranging from routine exams to complex surgical procedures.
